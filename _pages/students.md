# Join Us / 招生信息

## 🧭 研究方向 Research Areas

本课题组主要研究方向包括：

- 计算流体力学（CFD）与高精度数值模拟  
- 人工智能与流体力学结合（AI + CFD）  
- 液氢储运与氢能系统  
- 深海采矿多相流  

We are working on:

- Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and high-fidelity simulations  
- AI for fluid mechanics (AI + CFD)  
- Liquid hydrogen and hydrogen energy systems  
- Multiphase flow in deep-sea mining  

---

## 🎯 我们正在寻找的学生 Ideal Candidates

我们更倾向于招收以下类型的学生：

- 对科研有明确兴趣，并愿意长期投入  
- 具备较强的自驱力（能主动推进工作，而不是被动完成任务）  
- 有良好的逻辑思维和问题拆解能力  
- 具备团队合作意识，同时能够独立思考  

We are looking for students who:

- Are genuinely interested in research and willing to commit long-term  
- Are self-motivated and proactive  
- Have strong analytical and problem-solving skills  
- Can work both independently and collaboratively  

---

## ⚙️ 基础要求 Requirements

- 具备一定编程能力（Python / C++ 至少一种）  
- 有数值模拟 / CFD / 数学建模基础  
- 能阅读英文文献  

Minimum requirements:

- Programming skills (Python or C++)  
- Background in numerical simulation or CFD  
- Ability to read academic papers in English  

---

## ⭐ 优先考虑 Preferred Background

满足以下条件的申请者将被优先考虑：

- 有 CFD 软件经验（Fluent / OpenFOAM 等）  
- 有机器学习 / 深度学习经验  
- 有高性能计算（GPU / 并行计算）经验  
- 有科研项目或论文经验  

Preferred qualifications:

- Experience with CFD tools (Fluent, OpenFOAM)  
- Background in machine learning or deep learning  
- Experience in high-performance computing  
- Prior research experience or publications

## 🚫 不太适合的情况（很重要）
