## 👋 About

I am a Professor at Wuhan University of Technology. My research focuses on computational and data-driven approaches for complex fluid dynamics problems in marine and energy engineering.

My work aims to develop efficient numerical methods and integrate artificial intelligence techniques to address multi-scale and multi-physics flow problems.

## 👤 Biography

Maoqiang Jiang is a Professor at the School of Naval Architecture, Ocean and Energy Power Engineering, Wuhan University of Technology.

He received his B.E. degree in Process Equipment and Control Engineering from Taiyuan University of Technology, and his M.S. degree in Chemical Process Machinery from Zhejiang University. He obtained his Ph.D. in Thermal Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

From 2010 to 2017, he worked as an engineer at WISDRI Engineering & Research Incorporation Limited, gaining extensive experience in industrial engineering design. After that, he joined Huazhong University of Science and Technology as a Ph.D. student, focusing on computational fluid dynamics and multiphase flow.

During his doctoral studies, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at Cornell University (USA) from 2019 to 2020, supported by the China Scholarship Council. He later conducted research at the National University of Singapore (NUS) as a research fellow from 2021 to 2022, working on multiphase flow and high-performance computing. From 2021 to 2023, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

He joined Wuhan University of Technology in 2023 and is currently a Professor. His research focuses on high-fidelity numerical simulation and data-driven modeling for complex fluid dynamics problems, including particle-laden flows, multiphase systems, hydrogen energy systems, and deep-sea mining transport processes.

His work has been published in leading journals such as *Journal of Computational Physics*, *Physics of Fluids*, *Computer Physics Communications*, and *Nature Communications*. His research integrates advanced numerical methods, artificial intelligence, and high-performance computing to address engineering challenges in marine and energy systems.

In addition to research, he is actively involved in teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in thermodynamics and heat transfer, and is committed to integrating research and education.

## 👤 个人简介

江茂强，教授，现任武汉理工大学船海与能源动力工程学院教师。

2003年本科毕业于太原理工大学过程装备与控制工程专业，2010年获浙江大学化工过程机械硕士学位，2021年获华中科技大学热能工程博士学位。2010年至2017年在中冶南方工程技术有限公司从事工程设计工作，具有扎实的工程背景。

博士期间及之后，围绕计算流体力学与多相流动开展系统研究。2019年至2020年获国家留学基金委资助赴美国康奈尔大学开展联合培养研究，2021年至2022年在新加坡国立大学机械工程系从事科研工作。2021年至2023年在华中科技大学开展博士后研究。

2023年加入武汉理工大学船海与能源动力工程学院，现为教授。

主要研究方向包括：

- 计算流体力学（CFD）与高精度数值模拟  
- 人工智能与流体力学耦合（AI + CFD）  
- 液氢储运与氢能系统  
- 深海采矿多相流  

在浸没边界法、格子Boltzmann方法及其并行计算等方面开展了系统研究，相关成果发表于 Journal of Computational Physics、Physics of Fluids、Computer Physics Communications、Nature Communications 等国际期刊。

研究面向海洋工程与能源动力领域的实际需求，在深海采矿输运、多相流模拟以及氢能系统等方面具有重要应用价值。

同时承担本科生与研究生课程教学任务，致力于科研与教学融合，培养学生的工程分析能力与创新能力。
